About Us

Our Mission

At Abune Melke Tsedek School, our mission is to provide a high-quality education that inspires and empowers students to reach their full potential. We are committed to fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment that supports academic excellence and personal growth.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution known for its innovation, excellence, and commitment to student success. We strive to create a dynamic learning community where students are prepared to thrive in a global society.

Our History

Abune Melke Tsedek School was established in 1999 EC. with the goal of providing quality education to students in Wolkite. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, continually enhancing our programs and facilities to meet the changing needs of our students and community.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethics in all that we do.
  • Respect: We value and honor the diverse perspectives and backgrounds of our students and staff.
  • Excellence: We are committed to achieving excellence in teaching, learning, and personal development.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of teamwork and community to achieve our goals and support one another.
