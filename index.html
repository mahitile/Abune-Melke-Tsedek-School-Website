WELCOME TO ABUNE MELKE TSEDEQ PRIMARY AND GENERAL SECONDARY SCHOOL

We are delighted to welcome you to Ethiopia Orthodox Tewahdo Church Mihur Eyesus Monastery Abune Melke Tsedeq Primary And General Secondary school, where we foster a nurturing environment dedicated to academic excellence and personal growth. Explore our site to learn more about our programs, activities, and community.

Our Mission

At Abune Melke Tsedeq School, our mission is to provide a holistic education that empowers students to excel academically, socially, and emotionally. We strive to create a supportive learning environment that encourages curiosity, respect, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our Vision

At Abune Melke Tsedeq school, we envision a learning community where every student is inspired to reach their full potential. We strive to cultivate a nurturing environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking skills. Our aim is to empower students to become lifelong learners, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens. Through innovative teaching methods, personalized support, and a diverse range of opportunities.
